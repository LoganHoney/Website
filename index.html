Innovate (verb): to introduce new or improved ideas, methods, or products.

Never stop improving. I believe that if we stop moving forward, we actually start to move backwards. We should always be looking for opportunities to make our processes smoother, our relationships stronger, and our lives better. We have to act on the ideas that innovate because:

"Ideation without execution is delusion"
                                                                - Robin Sharma

Elevate (verb): to lift something or someone up.

Standards are meant to be raised, it is a form of innovation. I beleive we should always elevate our own performance. When we elevate or performace and hold ourselves to a higher standard than others, exceptional work occurs. When we do this, we start to influence those around us for the better.

"Excellence is contageous, so is mediocricy"
